Introduction

Born in the city of Kolkata, I have always been an introvert who prefers to live in her own personal space. My mom was always painting on unexpected objects like vases and mugs. While witnessing her make art with patience, I was always encouraged. When I was about eight years old, I recognised myself having a particular set of interest in art and craft. I enjoy art because it allows us to express ourselves. I could communicate through that expressiveness by relying on our own unique emotions, thoughts, and experiences.

My art journey and how I got into sketching...

Initially, I started making watercolour paintings in 2010, and up until 2019, I mainly focused on them. I was able to isolate myself and focus on my work in 2020 when Covid-19 put the entire globe on lockdown. It allowed me to develop artistic abilities in ways I never would have thought possible. I experimented with making colour sketches, black and white sketches, acrylic paintings, and oil paintings. In the present day, not only I make art, but I also have made it my passion as a source of income. Yup! That is me! And thank you for visiting my art website. Additionally, you can follow my account & my entire journey as a self-taught and self-made artist on Instagram at @artsbykash.
