Painting with watercolours

If not the oldest style of painting, watercolour paint is certainly among the oldest types of artwork.The name "watercolor" also refers to a piece of art created using this medium. Watercolor is a pigment ground in gum, typically gum arabic, and applied with a brush and water to a painting surface, typically paper.
