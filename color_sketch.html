Drawing with coloured pencils

Drawing with coloured pencils has a lot of appealing qualities. All you really need to get started producing with them is a few coloured pencils and some paper. You don't require dirty or dangerous chemicals, the materials are lightweight and portable, and preparation time and cleaning are almost non-issues. Additionally, coloured pencil drawing may produce incredibly fine-tuned and exquisite works of art that are on par with those produced with any other media.
